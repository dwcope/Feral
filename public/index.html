A Feral Coder

Having been born at too young an age, I was abandoned by my mum in the Australian Outback. A short time later, I was snatched by dingoes and hauled to their austere digs. They took great care to make me feel at home and I was soon looked upon as just another of the pack. Those were the good years -- we roamed free, masters of all we could see, washing down great gobbets of 'roo with flagons of Foster's.
